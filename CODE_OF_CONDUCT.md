# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors and maintainers of Ghumle are expected to keep collaboration respectful, direct, and useful. That includes:

- discussing ideas and code without personal attacks
- assuming good intent until behavior shows otherwise
- keeping feedback specific, technical, and actionable
- avoiding harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or abuse

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in this repository or associated project spaces:

- personal insults or hostile escalation
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- sexualized language or unwelcome advances
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- repeated off-topic disruption, spam, or bad-faith argumentation

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or block users when behavior harms collaboration or safety.

## Reporting

If you encounter a problem, follow the reporting path in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for sensitive concerns or open an appropriate GitHub report for non-sensitive moderation issues.
